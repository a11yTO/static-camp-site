#a11yTOCamp

November 18, 2017

Toronto, Ontario

Opening the doors for a full day of accessibility learning.

The Event

Camp is about making friends, sharing ideas, and growing our community. #a11yTO Camp encourages fresh voices to join in conversation with industry leaders. We welcome new ideas and seek to provide a diverse schedule.

#a11yTO believes in introducing digital accessibility to new people all year round through our monthly Meetups and at Camp. This year we’re riding the crest of our inaugural #a11yTO Conference and we want to build on the momentum. #a11yTO Conf was the pinnacle event of 2017 and we’re already planning our move to the next level and making 2018 #a11yTO’s most impactful year ever.
