The Event

Calling all campers. Get ready to spend a day learning from, and networking with, the greatest accessibility community around. That’s right, #a11yTO Camp is back for 2026. Join us Saturday, May 9th at the League Offices in beautiful downtown Toronto.

Due to limited capacity for this year’s event, to curb the number of no-shows, we will be charging a nominal amount to secure your ticket. Your $20 entrance fee will go towards our expected costs for ASL and Captioning, as well as lunch.

Tickets are still available.

Thank you and we hope to see you all in Toronto this May.

The #a11yTO Team
