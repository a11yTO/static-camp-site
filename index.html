The Event

We are currently scouting locations for #a11yTO Camp. As you can imagine, with things starting to open back up, it’s been a little difficult to secure a venue but fear not, we’ll get that sorted out in the coming weeks.

In the meantime, we’d like to share some information about an exciting format change to #a11yTO Camp. Starting this year, we’re going to back to our roots and running #a11yTO Camp event as a traditional BarCamp with a single track, shorter talks, and lots of opportunities to engage with the community.

The #a11yTO Camp call for presentations will open shortly and you’ll get your chance to vote for the talks that interest you. Stay tuned for more info!
