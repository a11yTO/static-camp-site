The Event

For those looking for information regarding the 2022 event, please refer to the archived site.

Camp is about making friends, sharing ideas, and growing our community. #a11yTO Camp encourages fresh voices to join in conversation with industry leaders. We welcome new ideas and seek to provide a diverse schedule.

#a11yTO believes in introducing digital accessibility to new people all year round through our monthly Meetups, at Camp, and our Conference.

We’re going back to our roots and running #a11yTO Camp as a traditional BarCamp with a single track, shorter talks, and lots of opportunities to engage with the community.
