The Event

#a11yTO Camp Returns...

Calling all campers. Get ready to spend a day learning from, and networking with, the greatest accessibility community around. That’s right, #a11yTO Camp is back for 2025. Join us Saturday, April 26th at the League Offices in beautiful downtown Toronto.

Due to limited capacity for this year’s event, to curb the number of no-shows, we will be charging a nominal amount to secure your ticket. Your $20 entrance fee will go towards our expected costs for ASL and Captioning, as well as lunch.

Our call for presentation submissions is now closed.

Thank you and we hope to see you all in Toronto this April.

The #a11yTO Team
